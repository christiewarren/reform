PROJECT PRESENTATIONS

The theme of our senior capstone show, Re/Form, focuses on how we have shared the basic foundation of our education and then reinterpreted it to form our own perspectives as designers. Our self-directed senior projects embody our unique interests, styles, skills, and ideas. Join us as we showcase how we have each translated our distinct design perspective into our individual projects.
