Re/Form

Every designer knows the grid, the structure that supports our work and lives. Re/Form embodies how we interpret that structure and how we adapt as structure changes. It’s our way of showcasing the foundation of our education and how we have built upon that foundation.

Typography

We reference the grid through a monospace typeface with geometric details.

Color Scheme

Drawing from Swiss design—and its influence on the grid—we combined primary colors with neons to create a vibrant and exciting color scheme.

Manipulation

Throughout the exhibition, we each manipulate and add to the grid to reflect our individual strengths and problem solving capabilities.

Presenting Us to You

Showcasing our skills and portfolios to you was the driving purpose behind every decision. We didn’t just want to tell you what we’ve learned; we wanted to show you.
